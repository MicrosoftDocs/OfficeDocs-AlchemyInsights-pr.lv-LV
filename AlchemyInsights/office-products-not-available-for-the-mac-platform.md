# <a name="office-products-not-available-for-the-mac-platform"></a>Office produkti, kas nav pieejami Mac platformai

Visio, Project, Publisher un Access nav pieejamas Mac platformai, tomēr Visio Online un Project Online ir pieejami un darbojas visās platformās.
