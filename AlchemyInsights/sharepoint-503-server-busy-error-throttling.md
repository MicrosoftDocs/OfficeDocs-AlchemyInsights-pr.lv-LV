<p><span data-ttu-id="7051e-103"><span style="mso-bidi-font-family: Calibri; mso-bidi-theme-font: minor-latin;">Lietotāji var saņemt 503 serveris ir aizņemts kļūda mēģinot orientēties uz Sharepoint vai OneDrive vietām.</span></span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="7051e-103"><span style="mso-bidi-font-family: Calibri; mso-bidi-theme-font: minor-latin;">Users may receive a 503 server is busy error when attempting to navigate to Sharepoint or OneDrive sites. </span></span></span></p> <p><span data-ttu-id="7051e-104"><span style="mso-bidi-font-family: Calibri; mso-bidi-theme-font: minor-latin;">Šī kļūda var izraisīt droselēšanas Sharepoint pakalpojumu ietvaros. SharePoint Online lieto droselēšanas uzturēt optimālu veiktspēju un uzticamību SharePoint Online pakalpojumiem. Droselēšanas ierobežojumus lietotāja darbībām vai vienlaicīgā pieslēguma numurs aicina (ar skriptu vai kods), lai izvairītos no resursu pārmērīgas. SharePoint Online uses throttling to maintain optimal performance and reliability of the SharePoint Online service. Throttling limits the number of user actions or concurrent calls (by script or code) to prevent overuse of resources. If you do get throttled, 99% of the time it is because of custom code.</span></span></span></p> <p><span data-ttu-id="7051e-105"><span style="mso-bidi-font-family: Calibri; mso-bidi-theme-font: minor-latin;">Lai iegūtu vairāk informācijas par droselēšanas skat. <a href="https://docs.microsoft.com/en-us/sharepoint/dev/general-development/how-to-avoid-getting-throttled-or-blocked-in-sharepoint-online">nepieļaut kļūst mitināts vai bloķēts SharePoint Online</a>.</span></span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="7051e-105"><span style="mso-bidi-font-family: Calibri; mso-bidi-theme-font: minor-latin;">For more information on throttling see, <a href="https://docs.microsoft.com/en-us/sharepoint/dev/general-development/how-to-avoid-getting-throttled-or-blocked-in-sharepoint-online">Avoid getting throttled or blocked in SharePoint Online</a>.</span></span></span></p> <p><span data-ttu-id="7051e-106"><span style="mso-bidi-font-family: Calibri; mso-bidi-theme-font: minor-latin;">Ja jūs uzskatāt, ka šī kļūda ir saistīta ar droselēšanas, jūs varat pārbaudīt, ja ir aktīva uzturēšana notiek uz jūsu īrnieks ar navigāciju uz <a href="https://portal.office.com/adminportal/home#/MessageCenter">īsziņu centru</a>. Visbeidzot, nodrošināt jūs apmeklējat <a href="https://portal.office.com/adminportal/home#/servicehealth">Veselības pakalpojumu</a> lapu, lai meklētu visus padomus/incidentiem, kas var būt sastopami.</span></span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="7051e-106"><span style="mso-bidi-font-family: Calibri; mso-bidi-theme-font: minor-latin;">If you believe this error is unrelated to throttling, you can check if there is active maintenance occurring on your tenant by navigating to the <a href="https://portal.office.com/adminportal/home#/MessageCenter">Message center</a>. Finally , ensure you visit the <a href="https://portal.office.com/adminportal/home#/servicehealth">Service Health</a> page to check for any advisories/incidents that may be occurring.</span></span></span></p> <p>&nbsp;</p>
